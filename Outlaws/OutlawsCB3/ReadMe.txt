OUTLAWS CYBERBOARD GAMEBOX NOTES
================================


ROLLING THE DICE:

Roll 1d6 in the normal way and cross-reference the result on the Dice Chart.


WEAPON COUNTERS:

These are not double-sided.  Use the "Empty" marker to mark a gun as empty.


RELOAD TIMES:

These can be found printed to the right of the Dice Chart.
